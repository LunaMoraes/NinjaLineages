## ✨ Overview

**Ninja Lineages** adds selectable lineage traits to Project Zomboid, each with its own combat role, passive identity, and special mechanics.

Choose a bloodline and survive the Knox Event with abilities inspired by legendary shinobi clans.

---

## 🧬 Lineages

| Lineage | Role | Core Fantasy |
|---|---|---|
| **Byakugan** | Precision / awareness | Superior perception and Gentle Fist combat |
| **Sharingan** | Evasion / evolution | Tomoe progression, dodge chance, and Mangekyo Kamui |
| **Senju** | Endurance / battlefield control | Life force, stamina recovery, and Wood Release |
| **Uzumaki** | Preparation / logistics | Fuinjutsu seals for alarms and storage |
| **Rinnegan** | High-power active ability | Shinra Tensei crowd disruption |

---

## 👁️ Byakugan

The **Byakugan** lineage focuses on awareness, perception, and close-range precision.

### Features

- Enhanced sight and hearing.
- Improved foraging awareness.
- Gentle Fist-inspired combat effects.
- Designed as a tactical melee-focused lineage.

---

## 🔴 Sharingan

The **Sharingan** evolves through combat experience.

### Tomoe Progression

| Stage | Requirement | Effect |
|---|---:|---|
| **1 Tomoe** | 1 zombie kill | Unlocks basic Sharingan dodge |
| **2 Tomoe** | 100 zombie kills | Improved dodge chance |
| **3 Tomoe** | 500 zombie kills | Maximum tomoe dodge chance |

### Mangekyo Sharingan

After reaching advanced Sharingan progression, the player can awaken **Mangekyo Sharingan** and access:

### Kamui

- Temporary phasing-style ability.
- Defensive escape tool.
- Costs endurance.
- Applies vision strain after use.
- Uses cooldown-based balancing.

---

## 🌳 Senju

The **Senju** lineage focuses on raw vitality and battlefield control.

### Features

- Strong endurance recovery.
- High survivability under pressure.
- Wood Release-inspired combat utility.

### Wood Release: Binding Roots

Senju can control space with roots that disrupt nearby zombies.

- Area control ability.
- Staggers or knocks down affected zombies.
- Costs endurance based on affected targets.
- Designed for horde control rather than burst damage.

---

## 🔶 Uzumaki

The **Uzumaki** lineage focuses on preparation, utility, and fuinjutsu.

### Alarm Seal

Place a seal to warn you when danger approaches.

Useful for:

- Base defense.
- Sleeping safety.
- Looting buildings.
- Perimeter awareness.

### Storage Seal

Use sealing techniques to store and transport items more efficiently.

Useful for:

- Loot runs.
- Long expeditions.
- Emergency supply kits.
- Base relocation.

---

## 🟣 Rinnegan

The **Rinnegan** is a high-cost lineage focused on overwhelming active power.

### Shinra Tensei

Unleash a repulsive force around the player.

- Pushes and disrupts nearby zombies.
- Can knock enemies down.
- Costs endurance.
- Has cooldown balancing.
- Best used as an emergency crowd-control tool.

---

## 🎮 Controls

Ninja Lineages adds its own ability access through in-game interaction systems.

Depending on the ability and current state, powers may be available through:

- Context menu.
- Radial menu.
- Custom keybinds.

Check the in-game keybind menu for **Ninja Lineages** controls.
